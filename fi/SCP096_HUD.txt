Tee yhteistyötä <color=#F00>SCP-tiimisi</color> kanssa. Aloitat rauhoittuneena, etkä voi hyökätä. Rankaise sinua katsoneita ihmisiä päästämällä raivosi valloilleen ja hyökkää heidän kimppuunsa raa'alla tavalla.
Try Not to Cry:When in the Docile State, press [key] on a flat wall or door to lean against it. While leaning, your cries are hushed and your Hume Shield regeneration rate is doubled.
Melee Attack:When in the Enraged state, press [key] to unleash a strong melee attack on nearby humans and doors. This attack cannot damage humans who have not looked at your face.
Charge:When in the Enraged state, press [key] to propel yourself at extreme speeds in a straight line. This attack deals massive damage to humans.

Scopophobia:You begin in a Docile state. In this state, you are unable to attack and move around slowly. If a human looks at your face, you gain the ability to enter the Enraged state. When Enraged, you move rapidly and gain powerful attacks, but can only see players who have looked at you.
